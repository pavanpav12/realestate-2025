Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to pay full amount before registration? +
No. You only pay the booking advance first. The remaining amount can be paid through bank loan or installment plan before registration.
Can I get a home loan for this property? +
Yes. Our properties are approved by major banks. We also help you with documentation and loan approval process.
Is site visit available on weekends? +
Yes, site visits are available all 7 days. You can schedule a visit by contacting our agent or booking online.
What documents are required for booking? +
You need Aadhar card, PAN card, and address proof. For loan, bank statements and salary slips may also be required.
Are there any hidden charges? +
No hidden charges. All costs including registration, GST and maintenance are explained clearly before booking.
